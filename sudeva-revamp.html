Our Philosophy

BELIEVE.
TRAIN.
RISE.

Founded in 2014 by Anuj Gupta and Vijay Hakari, Sudeva was built on a singular obsession: to give India's most talented footballers the infrastructure and opportunities they deserve — regardless of where they come from or what they can afford.



01
Residential Excellence

India's first full-residential football school in Delhi-NCR. A 24/7 environment for physical, technical, and personal growth.

02
Pan-India Scouting

60–75 days per year on the ground — tribal belts, hill states, coastal cities, and metros. Financial background is never a barrier.

03
International Exposure

Bundesliga partnership, Spanish club ownership, Premier League Next Gen Cup, and international training camps.

04
Education & Character

Football is the vehicle. Human development is the mission. Every Sudevan graduates a stronger individual.

Legacy & Milestones

A DECADE OF FIRSTS

🏆
I-League Debut

First and only club from Delhi in Hero I-League, debuting in 2020–21 with a fully all-Indian squad.

⚽
FIFA World Cup Venue

Sudeva's pitch was selected as an official training venue for the FIFA U-17 World Cup 2017, hosted in India.

🌍
European Club Ownership

First Indian football organization to acquire a European club — CD Olímpic de Xàtiva in Spain's third division.

🇩🇪
VfB Stuttgart Partnership

Strategic 3-year youth development partnership with Bundesliga club VfB Stuttgart, backed by Hindalco Industries.

🏅
Bayern Munich Recognition

Alumnus Shubho Paul selected as one of 15 global youth players by FC Bayern Munich's world squad programme.

🎓
AIFF Elite Accreditation

Sudeva's academy holds the prestigious 'Elite Category' accreditation from the All India Football Federation.
